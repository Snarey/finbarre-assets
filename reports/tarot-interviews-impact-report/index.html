About Tarot Interviews

Tarot Interviews is the world's first podcast interview format where creative people discuss their work using tarot cards as structured prompts.

The project brings writers, artists and other creative guests into conversation through tarot cards, using the cards not as prediction tools, but as a way to open up stories about work, memory, imagination and creative life.

Related work: Tarot DMs

Tarot DMs is the world's first epistolary tarot interview series: a messenger-based arts and culture interview format where writers, artists and creative people respond to three tarot prompts in a one-hour digital conversation.

Together, Tarot Interviews and Tarot DMs explore how creative work gets made, including the ideas, doubts, workarounds and turning points that rarely make it into conventional publicity material.
